    • Our Menu

    At Ginza Sushi, we embrace the rich heritage of Japan, presenting our delicacies in the spirit of traditional Omakase. Our menu is a tribute to the dedicated fishermen, farmers, and artisans, who, with their unwavering commitment to sustainable and eco-friendly practices, bring the finest produce from the sea and land. It's our privilege to serve you their bountiful yield, transformed into exquisite sushi by our skilled chefs.

    Bar Ginza

    At Ginza Sushi, we take pride in our exclusive Sake bar service, an embodiment of the rich Japanese brewing tradition. Our Sake selection is handpicked from the most dedicated brewers across Japan, who, through their commitment to sustainable methods and craft, produce this exceptional rice wine. Our expert sommeliers are at your service, guiding you through the fascinating journey of Sake tasting, pairing each variant with our sushi to enhance your dining experience.

    Private Events

    Ginza Sushi is delighted to offer private event hosting services. We are committed to providing an unforgettable experience, whether it's a corporate gathering, a family celebration, or an intimate dinner party. Our team of skilled professionals is dedicated to ensuring that every detail is meticulously planned and executed to your satisfaction.
